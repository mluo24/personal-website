This project is catered to the Pokémon fans, as it is specifically meant for people who are a bit more advanced in the games to keep track of the process of EV training their Pokémon to make them stronger.

The site calls <Link href="https://pokeapi.co/" underline="hover" target="_blank">PokéAPI</Link> to pull in-game location data as well as a few algorithms to determine shortest or most convenient "routes" for EV Training.
